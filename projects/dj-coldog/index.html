Phases

Phase 1

  • Phase 1 consists of making music off of templates. The Garageband DAW was utilized.

Phase 2

  • Phase 2 started with City Lights.
  • Phase 2 consists of exploring Garageband samples and putting them together to make music.

Phase 3

  • Phase 3 consists of the Highs and Lows Album released on SoundCloud.
  • Phase 3 has custom drum beats and some mixing.

Phase 4

  • Phase 4 started with the idea of Life in January 2020.
  • Phase 4 marked the start of DJ ColDog's rapping career.

Phase 5

  • Phase 5 started with the idea of The First Dog.
    • The First Dog consists of two features, Speedy C and Trevdog, fresh cooked beats, and out of this world bars.
